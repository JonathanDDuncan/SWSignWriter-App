SignWriter is a tool to transliterate text in several languages to signwriting notation and share them through social media.

To make use of it, first in settings you need to select a Puddle. There should be a Puddle corresponding to every SignLanguage.

Next, you can go the Edit and start writing what you want to transliterate to SignWriting. If you are not used to speak in sign language, note that the structure of sentences is different from spoken languages. You should structure sentences and words on your own. SignWriter will transliterate your text by each word.

After that you can edit the length of your SignWriting text.

Finally, you can share an image of the text through the medium of your choice.

This app is in continuosly development. Translations of UI text will be completely added further.
